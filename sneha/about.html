Who We Are

Margadarshi is a non-governmental organization committed to supporting differently-abled individuals by providing them with access to education, skill development, and meaningful employment opportunities. Established in 1999, we have worked with thousands of individuals to help them lead empowered and independent lives.

Our Vision

To build an inclusive society where every individual, regardless of their abilities, can achieve their full potential and contribute meaningfully to the community.

Our Mission

  • To provide quality education and vocational training to differently-abled individuals
  • To offer therapeutic services and emotional support
  • To facilitate employment opportunities through partnerships with businesses
  • To spread awareness and promote advocacy for disability rights
  • To support families and caregivers with resources and counseling

Impact

Over the past two decades, Margadarshi has:

  • Helped over 5,000 individuals gain access to inclusive education
  • Enabled 2,000+ people to secure sustainable jobs
  • Conducted 500+ community outreach programs and workshops
  • Supported more than 3,000 families with resources and training

We believe in equal opportunity for all. Join us in creating a better, more inclusive world.
